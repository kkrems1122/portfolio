        <div id="about-deftones">
          <p>Deftones is an American alternative metal band formed in Sacramento, California, in 1988. Known for their unique blend of heavy guitar riffs, ethereal atmospheres, and emotive vocals, Deftones have garnered critical acclaim and a dedicated fan base over the years. Their music often explores themes of inner turmoil, existentialism, and emotional catharsis, creating a sound that's both aggressive and introspective. With albums like "White Pony," "Around the Fur," and "Diamond Eyes," Deftones have established themselves as influential figures in the alternative metal genre, consistently pushing boundaries and evolving their sound.</p>
        </div>
        <div id="about-chino">
          <p>Chino Moreno is the lead vocalist and occasional guitarist for the American alternative metal band Deftones. Born on June 20, 1973, Moreno is known for his distinctive vocal style, seamlessly shifting between aggressive screams and haunting melodies. His lyrical content often delves into themes of personal struggles, introspection, and emotional intensity, contributing to the band's dark and atmospheric sound. Beyond Deftones, Moreno has been involved in various side projects, showcasing his versatility as a musician. With his dynamic stage presence and powerful voice, Moreno has solidified himself as one of the most iconic figures in the alternative metal scene.</p>
        </div>
